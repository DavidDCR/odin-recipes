Pasta Sauce

Description

A classic, versatile homemade tomato sauce. Great as base for other pasta sauces or on its own.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • ½ tsp dried oregano. basil or mixed herbs
  • ½ tsp castor sugar

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and fry the onion over a low heat for five minutes, stirring regularly with a wooden spoon. Add the garlic and cook for a few seconds more.
  2. Add the tomatoes, herbs and sugar and cook for five minutes, stirring regularly.
  3. Stir in a splash of water to loosen the sauce, if needed, and season to taste.
